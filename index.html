About Me.

Meet Alriz Fajardo, a down-to-earth individual hailing from the vibrant province of Bulacan, Philippines. With a dynamic entrepreneurial journey under his belt, Alriz has left an indelible mark in the business world. As the visionary behind a thriving clothing enterprise, he achieved exceptional sales, tallying an impressive 35,000 units in just four years. Additionally, his venture into Chicken By Product Trading proved equally fruitful, with sales reaching a remarkable 90,000 kilograms over three years. Today, Alriz pours his heart into his true passion: video editing. Armed with over five years of hands-on experience, he has fine-tuned his skills to perfection, seamlessly weaving magic into every frame. From raw footage to polished masterpieces, Alriz's commitment to excellence shines through, ensuring each project exceeds expectations. His ultimate goal? To not just entertain but to captivate audiences and drive tangible results for his valued clients.

In a world where attention spans are shrinking by the second, I don't just edit videos—I craft stories that hook viewers from the very first frame. With 5+ years of experience in Premiere Pro and After Effects, I specialize in turning raw footage into polished, high-impact content that keeps audiences watching till the end. Whether it's a fast-paced promo, cinematic vlog, or smooth motion graphics, I know how to make every second count.
